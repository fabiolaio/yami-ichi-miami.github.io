WELCOME TO YAMI-ICHI.MIAMI

GM. Calling all artists! The AI Internet Yami-Ichi is a black flea market for selling physical artwork inspired by the internet, where people deal with “Internet-ish” items in real space. We are now accepting submissions for The AI internet Yami-Ichi on March 12, at HODLER Gallery in Miami. We are seeking artists who are pushing the boundaries of AI art with physical exhibition pieces and NFTs. If you have a unique and innovative take on internet culture and want to showcase it to a wider audience, we want to hear from you. If you are interested in submitting your artwork we have 3 types of submissions:

  • Artists with Physical AI Art that will be exhibited. Do you have any physical AI Art that you would like to exhibit?(not screens, we are focused in showcasing objects, paintings, photos, etc) We will have a few spots to display physical art.
  • Artists with AI NFTS will be displayed in one of our 8 screens. All accepted artworks will be part of our AI Livestream through AI24.
  • Artists that want to sell physical AI artwork linked to their NFTs, do you have anything like that? DM us on twitter!.

We will have a curatorial team to decide which artwork we are going to display. Submit your work now for the chance to be a part of this exciting event! Please fill out the Typeform to submit your work and keep reading for the full guidelines and join our AI Yami-Ichi Community on Twitter.

**NOTE Artworks in JPG OR MP4, submit your social media, your artwork and a link to your NFT, keep your shareD settings open cause we are not requesting access.

We do not cover the shipping of physical artwork, we promise we will take care of it, mount it and send it back (this shipping cost is also on you). We do not take any percentage of the sell, this is completely for you, deadline for the artwork March 10.

*If you are a local artist from Miami you can submit any artwork related to the internet, reach me out and let us know!
